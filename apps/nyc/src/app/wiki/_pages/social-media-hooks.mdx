A grab bag of hook formulas for music posts. Fill in the blanks.

## 1. "Picture this, except with ___"

*Example:* "Picture this, except with double the bass and zero sleep."

## 2. "This is for the ___ who ___"

*Example:* "This is for the indie kids who treat reverb like a love letter."

## 3. "Just sitting here waiting for the people who ___ to stumble onto my page"

*Example:* "Just sitting here waiting for the people who do their best writing at 3am to stumble onto my page."

## 4. "Tag someone who ___"

*Example:* "Tag someone who needs a soundtrack for an empty highway at midnight."

## 5. "What would it sound like if ___ had ___? Probably something like this."

*Example:* "What would it sound like if a Taylor Swift love song had an ending where he bolted out the door? Probably something like this."

## 6. "In my head, this is what ___ sounds like"

*Example:* "In my head, this is what falling for a stranger in a gas station parking lot at 2am sounds like."

## 7. "Feels like the lovechild of ___ and ___"

*Example:* "Feels like the lovechild of The 1975 and Tame Impala."

## 8. "What movie does this belong in? I'm betting on ___"

*Example:* "I'm betting on a coming-of-age road trip movie."

## 9. "Try not to fixate on the ___, but ___"

*Example:* "Try not to fixate on the bassline, but you're going to scrub back at least twice."
