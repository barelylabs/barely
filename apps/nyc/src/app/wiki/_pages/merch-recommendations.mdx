## Apparel

### Sizing

A good starting point for shirt & hoodie size distributions:

- S: 10%
- M: 24%
- L: 32%
- XL: 24%
- XXL: 10%

I've found that in-person (gig) sales trend more towards S & M, whereas online cart sales trend more towards XL & XXL. You'll get an idea after the first 100 sales what your specific distribution looks like.

### Blanks

- Gildan Hammer tees are a medium-quality option (~$5/blank shirt @100 units)
- Comfort Colors tees are a good option on the higher quality end (~$7/blank shirt @100 units)

### Pricing

When possible, I recommend working with a local screen printer. For reference, my own band was recently able to get a run of 100 Comfort Colors single-color prints for $12/shirt ([Bushwick Print Lab](https://bushwickprintlab.org/)).
